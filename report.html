Professional Practices and Design Quality

  1. We have organized all the files in their respective directories
  2. We used a framework which had repeated styles in the main.css file, and orders.css since the orders needed specific styling
  3. Our content is neatly aligned, with most of it favoring the left and taking adequate space of the page
  4. The text is spaced with a good amount of padding to make sure not text bleeds over
  5. We used consisten formatting with kebab-case
  6. Our site is very accessable with frequent use of alt tags accross all images and simply outlined data for screen readers to go over
  7. all our files are lowercase and have alphanumeric characters
